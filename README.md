# Envision Partner Review

High-level partner pitch deck for meetings with research institutions, manufacturers, and enterprise partners.

## Structure

12-slide hosted presentation covering:

1. Title: Built to Partner
2. At a Glance: six-stat band
3. What We Do Today: four capabilities intro
4. Textile &amp; Sewn-Goods Manufacturing: core competency
5. Innovation Lab, Kitting, Quality: capabilities continued
6. The Story: textile to funded federal R&amp;D engagement intro
7. CWHW Program: Army Cold Weather Hand Warmer with DEVCOM Soldier Center
8. The Arc That Matters: four-step capability-to-engagement pattern
9. Synergy with Research &amp; Innovation Partners
10. Synergy with Industry &amp; Manufacturing Partners
11. Synergy with Enterprise &amp; Ecosystem Partners
12. Strategic Direction + Close: four directional themes, Sebastian and Patrick contacts

## Customizing for a specific partner

The deck is intentionally generic so it can be reused across partner meetings. Two places typically get tailored per audience:

- Slide 1 title meta: update `Prepared For` to the partner's name.
- Slides 9 through 11: swap `syn-partner-label`, `syn-head`, `syn-body`, and the `syn-hooks` list items with partner-specific language if desired.
